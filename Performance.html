Flow Show is a hip-hop dance performance that takes place every school term at Queen's University. Students from Flow Dance Club have the opportunity to showcase what they've been working on during the term to friends and loved ones. KinetiQ, as well as other hip-hop dance clubs, have also been fortunate to showcase our work of art in the biggest hip-hop dance show here at Queen's University. The show offers a wide variety of entertainment; such as multiple classes' showcases, Flow Performance Crew, Step Crew, KinetiQ Crew, extra numbers, as well as a student freestyle cypher.
