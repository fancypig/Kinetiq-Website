The Breaking Clubs Aptitude Test, or BCAT, is a bboy jam that takes place every year in McMaster University during the winter term. Hosted by the McMaster Breaking Club, BCAT attracts numerous breaking clubs from all over Ontario universities to settle the age old question; who is the best university breaking club in Ontario.
